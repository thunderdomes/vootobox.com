    TripLingo

    TripLingo is the ultimate tool to help you overcome the language barrier when you travel. Learn essential phrases, translate your voice, talk to a live translator, and get a crash course on the local culture- all in the app that was named the “2012 Business Travel Innovation of the Year” by Fast Company.

    Don’t sweat over the local lingo before your next trip abroad. With TripLingo, travel easy and impress friends as you learn to talk like a local with our casual, fun and humorous approach to getting around foreign countries when you don't speak the language. Learn important phrases and slang, translate your voice (and understand the response!), or even talk with a live translator on demand. TripLingo’s award-winning platform includes:

  • Over 1200 phrases per language (including slang!)

  • Voice to voice translation

  • Access to a live translator

  • A 10,000 word offline dictionary

  • Customized phrase lists suited to your needs

  • Over 2000 high quality audio files per language

  • Built-in flashcards

  • A “culture crash course” to get familiar with local customs and etiquette

  • Much more!
